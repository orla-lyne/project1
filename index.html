ALBERT EINSTEIN

The man who developed relativism

Here's a time line of Albert Einstein

  • 1879 - Born

    March 14: Born in Ulm, Germany.

  • 1896 - Secondary Education Completed

    Graduates from Aargau Cantonal School in Switzerland.

  • 1900 - Graduation

    Graduates from the Swiss Federal Polytechnic in Zurich with a degree in physics.

  • 1902-1909 - Swiss Patent Office

    Works at the Swiss Patent Office in Bern while pursuing scientific research.

  • 1905 - Annus Mirabilis (Miracle Year)

    Publishes four groundbreaking papers, including the Special Theory of Relativity and Photoelectric Effect.

  • 1909 - Professor at University of Zurich

    Becomes a Professor at the University of Zurich.

  • 1914 - Moves to Berlin

    Moves to Berlin to join the Prussian Academy of Sciences and the University of Berlin.

  • 1915 - General Theory of Relativity

    Completes the General Theory of Relativity.

  • 1919 - Experimental Confirmation of General Relativity

    First experimental confirmation of General Relativity through a solar eclipse observation.

  • 1921 - Nobel Prize in Physics

    Wins the Nobel Prize in Physics for the Photoelectric Effect.

  • 1933 - Emigrates to the United States

    Emigrates to the U.S. due to the rise of Nazi Germany.

  • 1939 - Letter to President Roosevelt

    Co-signs a letter urging President Roosevelt to begin atomic bomb research, leading to the Manhattan Project.

  • 1940 - U.S. Citizenship

    Becomes a U.S. citizen.

  • 1952 - Offered Presidency of Israel

    Offered the presidency of Israel, which he declines.

  • 1955 - Death

    April 18: Dies in Princeton, New Jersey.

“Imagination is more important than knowledge. For knowledge is limited, whereas imagination embraces the entire world, stimulating progress, giving birth to evolution.”
- Albert Einstein
